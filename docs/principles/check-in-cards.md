# Principles: Check-In Cards

Lightweight pre-computed interactive activities inserted between explanation cards in Teach Me (Baatcheet + Explain). Warm comprehension checks — not quizzes. Check-ins are where real engagement lives.

## 1. Paired Light + Heavy

Each pair = one **light** activity (~5–10s, recall) + one **heavy** activity (~15–25s, analysis). Both test the same preceding 2–3 cards at different cognitive levels.

## 2. Frequency & Placement

A pair after every 2–3 content cards. Never before card 3. Never after the summary card. At least 2 content cards between pairs.

## 3. Variety

Mix different checkin card types so that students don't get bored.

## 4. Language

Follows [easy-english.md](easy-english.md): sentences under 12 words, no idioms, Indian contexts. Audio text is pure words — no symbols or markdown.

## 5. Feedback & Safety

Wrong → hint + TTS + shake animation. Correct → success message + TTS.
